About ILO2025

Hosted by the Stanford Logic Group under the guidance of Prof. Genesereth, the ILO contest aims to internationalize and expand regional logic contests for high school students worldwide.

This contest is based on the “Intro to Logic” material made openly available by the Stanford Logic Group, all student applicants must get familiar with the course material at *(http://logic.stanford.edu/intrologic/public/help.php) before the contest start date.

*(The ultimate phase of the contest will take place on-campus at the Stanford University campus)

Stanford University | Final-Round Contest Site

Rooted in Stanford’s rich history, this contest is a proactive effort to elevate global awareness of logic and reasoning. Going beyond geographical boundaries, the contest seeks to invite students worldwide to engage in a blend of online and offline competitions.

2024, marks the first year for Stanford Logic Group’s effort to expand the contest’s reach to encompass fundamental logic internationally and to offer students worldwide the opportunity to participate in the final competing round in person at Stanford Campus.
